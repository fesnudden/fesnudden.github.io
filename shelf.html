Plant shelf

2022

Personal projects

In my spare time I enjoy designing and making objects for a particular purpose, usually for around my flat. In this case, I wanted to find a way of raising my plant seedlings and cuttings up off the floor to expose them to more of the light coming through the French door. This small shelf unit was the result.
