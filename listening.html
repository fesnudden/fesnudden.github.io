Listening room

Madrid, Spain - 2017

Y3 Studio project, Erasmus year UEM, Spain

This project evolved from a series of drawing exercises created over several weeks within the SCAMPER framework. The resulting project is a space for members of the public to listen to vinyl in a space designed for ultimate audio experience. The drawings and the building form are strongly influenced by the reflection and absorption of sound; and this initiated a personal interest in acoustic design, and a query of why aesthetic is so heavily concentrated on when designing a building experience.
